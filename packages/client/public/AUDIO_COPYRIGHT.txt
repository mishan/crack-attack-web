Crack Attack! — Audio assets copyright and provenance
=====================================================

The music (music/*.ogg) and sound effects (sounds/*.wav) shipped here were
taken from Fedora's `crack-attack` package (the same set Arch distributes as
`crack-attack-sounds`, built from the Fedora source RPM
crack-attack-1.1.14-40.fc30). Upstream Crack Attack! ships no audio in its
`data/` tree; these files are fork-supplied and have been carried by the
Fedora/Arch packages for years. The Fedora package installs a combined
`music-sound-copyright.txt`; that file could not be retrieved directly (the
dist-git blocks automated fetches), so this notice reconstructs its two parts
from primary sources.

Two different licenses apply:
